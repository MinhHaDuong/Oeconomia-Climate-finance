# Journal survey: companion methods paper

## Paper scope

Computational approaches to the history of economic thought: multilingual bibliometric
corpus construction, sentence-transformer embeddings for thematic analysis, endogenous
periodization, co-citation community detection — applied to climate finance history.

Sits at intersection of: HPS methodology, scientometrics, digital humanities, STS.
